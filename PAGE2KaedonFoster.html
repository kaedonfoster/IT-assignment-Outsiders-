Plot Events


The plot of this story is basically how Johnny and Ponyboy run away to an abandoned church. We didn’t get any further than this, but I’m guessing it will continue with someone attacking them, or one of them getting hurt. At the beginning of the book, there was also a point when Ponyboy got attacked, but that's not much of a plot event. I am excited to continue reading the book and hoping that there is a lot of action to come.
